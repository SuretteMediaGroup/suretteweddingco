There are venues that photograph beautifully, and then there are venues that feel like they were built for the kind of day Heidi and Dan had. The Beauport Hotel sits right on the working harbour in Gloucester, Massachusetts — and on an August evening with that golden Atlantic light, every frame felt like it had been lit by the world itself.

Heidi and Dan chose an outdoor ceremony on the waterfront deck, framed by towering white floral arrangements that echoed the crests of the waves behind them. Heidi's square-neck gown was effortlessly elegant, and Dan's classic black tuxedo was made for the setting. There was a quietness to their ceremony — the kind that happens when two people are completely sure of each other.

The reception moved inside to sweeping harbour views, candlelight, and the kind of dancing that starts early and goes all night. These are the moments we live for — the laughter, the tears on the dance floor, the stolen glances that tell the whole story.

If you're planning a wedding at the Beauport Hotel or anywhere along the Massachusetts North Shore, we'd love to be your photographers.
